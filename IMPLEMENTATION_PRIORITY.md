## üöÄ Quick Start: Most Critical Features

Based on standard commercial property management in South Africa, here are the **must-have** features to implement first:

### Priority 1: Core Financial Operations (Week 1-2)

1. **Recurring Monthly Rent Invoicing**
   - Auto-generate invoices on 1st of month
   - Link to active leases
   - Include escalation if applicable

2. **VAT Calculations (15%)**
   - Add VAT fields to invoices
   - Calculate VAT automatically
   - Display VAT breakdown

3. **Payment Allocation**
   - Allocate payments to invoices
   - Handle partial payments
   - Update invoice status automatically

4. **Arrears Tracking**
   - Calculate overdue amounts
   - Age analysis (30/60/90 days)
   - Arrears reports

### Priority 2: Lease Management (Week 3)

5. **Lease Escalation**
   - Track escalation dates
   - Auto-calculate escalated rent
   - Escalation history

6. **Lease Renewal Reminders**
   - Email reminders (60/30/14 days before)
   - Renewal tracking

### Priority 3: Enhanced Reporting (Week 4)

7. **Financial Reports**
   - Income statements
   - Expense reports
   - P&L by property

8. **Tenant Statements**
   - Generate monthly statements
   - Include all transactions
   - PDF generation
